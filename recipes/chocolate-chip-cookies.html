Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Description:

What would be of the world without chocolate chip cookies? I can tell you something, it wouldn't be a happy one. Here's the catch though, everybody has a different ideal for the perfect chocolate chip coookies. Some people like them hard enough that it can break a teeth, other people people like it almost raw; but I prefer to think that most civilized people like the type that you can chew like a cow and feel fine about life.

The point I actually want to make clear here is, this is one of the many perfectly delicious chocolate chip cookies recipes. I don't guarantee you will like it, but you have to try it. I think this is part of the way for mutual understanding, you see. Important detail, an oven is a must. If you have one, great! You're minutes away from being able to brag about the best thing you have ever made. Yes, a cookie.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven at 350F or 175C.
  2. Put the butter, the white sugar and the brown sugar in a bowl and mix it with a mixer until it's smooth.
  3. Keep mixing while adding the eggs, one by one. Then add the vanilla extract.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water. Add to the batter along with the salt.
  5. Stir the flour and the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough in a greased pan, separate them considering they'll grow.
  7. Bake for at least 10 minutes.
